## Overview

**Pasandida Aurat** is an innovative dating platform that prioritizes authentic connections through anonymous questioning systems and "not going to lie" honesty features. Currently in development, the platform aims to revolutionize online dating by removing superficial barriers and encouraging genuine conversations.

## Key Features (In Development)

### **Authentic Connection System**

- **Anonymous Questions**: Ice-breaker questions without revealing identity
- **"Not Going to Lie" Feature**: Encouraging radical honesty
- **Gradual Reveal**: Progressive disclosure of personal information
- **Compatibility Matching**: Algorithm-based personality matching

### **Privacy-First Design**

- **Anonymous Interaction**: Initial conversations without photos
- **Secure Communication**: End-to-end encrypted messaging
- **Consent-Based Reveal**: User-controlled identity disclosure
- **Safety Reporting**: Comprehensive user protection system
